MASSIVE ATTACK

Mezzanine

God is looking down on everything we do as if he were above in the mezzanine, standing idle as we fall to the rules of his realm, too pretentious to help.

The more perverse your angle, the more imperfect it becomes; teasing and taunting us with strict rules.

We are insignificant.

Pictured is the Brookview Pool, a childhood favorite, two years abandoned.

The perfect surrealistic and decrepit location to visually embody the trip hop ground-zero, Mezzanine.

I wanted to stretch the definition of a mezzanine, I thought about the abandoned pool as a location where even on the mezzanine level, it still felt like someone unseen was watching.

I found this cool little clear vinyl record from a Dark Side of the Moon gate fold, which I thought would make an exquisite complement to my metallic cover.
