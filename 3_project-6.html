Buffalo Wild Wings

Unique, and Pretty Cool Looking.


Unique textures, materials, carrier shapes AND card shapes, for suggestion and presentation to the head honchos of B-Dubs, Sonic, Arby’s, and others.

Initally, we did just that: crazy packaging directions with unothodox card shapes, like this one, leveraging the iconic to-go basket shape in a miniature form to hold the gift card.

We stepped back a little on the crazy card shapes in later iterations, as they had to accommodate 2/3” mag strips.

We used unique materials, like a matte grascale ink paired with a gold metallic PVC and a familiar card shape with a slight wallet-defying twist.

This one uses a die-cut 82 to peek through to the card on the inside, with text reading “inside every fan”

And then under the gift card, when removed: “is an actual buffalo”

It was a stretch, but man would it be cool if you opened the card, and a golden #1 finger flipped out on 3D pop-up bleachers, surounded by gold foil stripes and a divine halo with a pure gold engraved Buffalo Wild Wings logo and an actual flash mob choir that sang when you opened it...



Okay maybe not, but pop-up bleachers seemed pretty cool to us. Uh, yes it actually pops up.

Some of them used clear PVC, like these.

Of course, there is still a regularly shaped gift card, complete with iridescent foil and an actual choir.

We showed each of these card directions with a visual and textual flex, flat and mocked up. As well as how they would live on their current gift card rack.

This was an initiative on behalf of Inspire Brands, the parent company of our beloved B-Dubs, so these card concepts have been taken and will be interpolated in-house into the designs that will be used in stores.
