During my first internship in the ChangeUp retail design practice, Spring of 2019, the responsibility was put on my shoulders when Wawa began considering a mural for their location in Gainesville.

This initiative was quickly put on hold after two or three iterations, though.



Fast forward eight months to the middle of my second semester with ChangeUp, Wawa reopened the mural project and was, once again, placed entirely on my shoulders.

Unfortunately, Wawa decided not to go with the texture in the final deliverable :(

(in case you were interested in which locations each of these represent I've got it layed out here)

I had an academic semester between this internship and the last, and consequently a lot of progress from myself as a designer, and this project truly put that in perspective, which was an enlightening and gratifying opportunity.







During my reasearch I saw one photo of a bunch of UF students standing in a circle around this sculpture of a potato and holding hands, that made me laugh a little bit.
