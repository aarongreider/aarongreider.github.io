During my first internship in the ChangeUp retail design practice, Spring 2019, the responsibility was put on my shoulders when Wawa began considering a mural for their location in Gainesville.

This initiative was quickly put on hold after two or three iterations, though.

Fast forward eight months to the middle of my second semester with ChangeUp, Fall 2019, Wawa reopened the mural project and was, once again, placed entirely on my shoulders.

During my research, I saw one photo of a bunch of UF students standing in a circle around this sculpture of a potato and holding hands, that made me laugh a little bit.
