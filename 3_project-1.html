What's a Homeshake?


Homeshake's Midnight Snack is a hypnogic bedroom pop masterwork, which I decided to adopt as a humorous metaphor, featuring myself... as the midnight snack...

I experimented with pixel wind filtering and the repeated overlay of imagery to achieve a graphic language as hypnotic and disorienting as the accompanying music.

I began my preparations early by forgetting leftover kraft mac n cheese in my refrigerator for *just* enough time to lose it's color and become a science experiment

I faced a dilemma upon entering the emptied refrigerator: risk expiring the mayo, or freeze it.

During Midnight Snack take two, I discovered the "pickled brain" effect of frozen-then-thawed mayonaise.

I planned my imagery to be conceptually uncanny,
