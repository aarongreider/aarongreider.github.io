Alley Fiend

Beginning with a category and a statement of intent, to a logo, then to the spread design, this academic project became very dense.

To consolidate, most of my iterations are contained in gifs.

If you're interested in the initial sketches, or an in-depth statement of my philosophy and voice behind the final and runner-up concepts, pull up this process document here.

Or scroll on for pretty pictures.

The ultimate sentiment of the publication is: we do what we want, we do crack, we do anarchy. Alternatively: we came, we saw, we anarchy.

And also we steal articles from The Onion. But this is an academic project so that's okay.

I visualized my anarcho-punk muse with dense textures formed with a typographical tribute to my home boy David Carson, and smearing actual dirt all over everything.
