BLOBERT’S MAGIC POLOROID SAVES THE WORLD.

Celluloid

Celluloid is a twisting coming of age story where a blob takes on the world, eventually coming to terms with his own faults and overcoming a labyrinth of delusional parallels to his subconscious as well as his own life, with the help of a magic Polaroid.

The hero is a blob, who is bored, his nemesis is an evil corporate boss who is threatening to destroy the world. On a fateful day, Blobert Ramone gets evicted from his sisters apartment after not paying rent, when he stumbles upon a magic Polaroid camera that will freeze anything he takes a picture of in time, forever.

The unnamed corporate boss has a machine with the potential to destroy all of the world’s ecosystems, and Blobert’s Polaroid camera is the only thing that can stop it.
