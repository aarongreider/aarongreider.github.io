Buffalo Wild Wings

Unique Materials and Carrier Shapes.


Unique textures, materials, carrier shapes AND card shapes, for suggestion and presentation to the head honchos of B-Dubs, Sonic, Arby’s, amongst others.

Initially, we did just that: crazy packaging directions with unorthodox card shapes, like the one above, leveraging the iconic to-go basket shape in a miniature form to hold the gift card.

We stepped back a little on the crazy card shapes in later iterations, as they had to accommodate 2/3” mag strips.

It was a stretch, but man would it be cool if you opened the card, and a golden #1 finger flipped out on 3D pop-up bleachers, surrounded by gold foil stripes and a divine halo with a pure gold engraved Buffalo Wild Wings logo and an actual flash mob choir that sang when you opened it...



Okay maybe not, but pop-up bleachers seemed pretty cool to us. Uh, yes it actually pops up.

Despite the unique shapes, there is still a regularly sized gift card, complete with iridescent foil and an actual choir.

We showed each of these card directions with a visual and textual flex, flat and mocked up. As well as how they would live on their current gift card rack.
