Mezzanine

God is looking down on everything we do as if he were above in the mezzanine, standing idle as we fall to the rules of his realm, as if he were too pretentious to help.

The more perverse your angle, the more imperfect it becomes; teasing and taunting us with strict rules.

We are insignificant.

Meanwhile at the abandonded local pool...

There's probably something surreal, supernatural, and maybe a little trippy.

Pictured is the Brookview Pool, a childhood favorite, two years abandoned.

The perfect surrealistic and decrepit location to visually embody the trip hop ground-zero, Mezzanine.

I wanted to stretch the definition of a mezzanine, and the abandoned pool served as location where you were on the mezzanine level, but still felt like someone unseen was watching.

I found this cool little clear vinyl record from a Dark Side of the Moon gate fold, which I thought would make an exquisite complement to my metallic cover.
